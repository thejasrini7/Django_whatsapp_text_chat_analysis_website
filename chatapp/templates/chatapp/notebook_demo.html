Notebook Demo

This page demonstrates the notebook side panel feature. Click the notebook icon in the bottom right to open it.

How to Use the Notebook

  1. Click the button in the bottom right corner to open the notebook
  2. Create multiple pages using the "+" button in the notebook sidebar
  3. Rename pages by double-clicking on their titles
  4. Pin important pages to keep them at the top
  5. Notes are automatically saved to your browser's localStorage
  6. Export pages as .txt or .md files using the export button
  7. Duplicate pages to create copies
  8. Delete pages you no longer need (you must keep at least one page)

Features

Auto-save

Your notes are automatically saved as you type, with a status indicator showing save progress.

Pin Pages

Pin important pages to keep them at the top of your notebook for quick access.

Export

Export any page as a .txt or .md file to save outside the browser.

Duplicate

Create copies of pages to experiment or organize your thoughts.

Privacy & Storage

All notes are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This means:

  • Your notes are private and never sent to any server
  • Notes persist even after closing the browser (unless you clear site data)
  • No account or login is required
  • Data is only accessible from this same browser and device

Note: If you clear your browser data or switch devices, your notes will not transfer.
